B Tech Admission under Govt. Quota

Fee Structure Govt. Quota seats will be allotted by the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE) based on KEAM 2022 Rank List.

College code for Govt.Quota is SNG.
For Govt. Quota , candidates have to apply online with the college code SNG and branch of preference as and when announced by the Commissioner for the Entrance Examination.

 

Admission Procedure 2022

1. Govt. allotted students should fill the application form which is available on the top of this page (Application Form Fee not required)

2. Upload the necessary documents in the prescribed format.

3. Submit all original documents (details below) and balance fees (Check SNGCE Govt. Fees Structure) at SNGCE office.

Documents to be submitted at the time of admission


» S.S.L.C. / 10th Mark sheet (Original + 2 copies)
» Qualifying Certificate (original + 3 copies)
       HSE - Mark list
       CBSE - Pass Certificate, Mark list, Migration Certificate
       (Outside Kerala) Pass Certificate, Mark lists, Migration Certificate

» Transfer Certificate from the Institution last attended
» Conduct Certificate from the Institution last attended
» Physical Fitness Certificate in the relevant format given in Annexure XVII (a) or XVII (b) of the KEAM (CEE) Prospectus(May be produced later, if not available now)

» Caste & Annual income Certificate from Village Officer/Tahsildar in the case of SC/ST/OEC/SEBC

» Entrance Exam. Data Sheet (Rank) (2 copies)
» Admit Card (Entrance Examination)
» Allotment Memo issued by the CEE
» Fee Receipt which paid to CEE
» Photo – 3 Nos.
» Aadhar card (1 copy)
» Proof for any special reservation if applicable.
