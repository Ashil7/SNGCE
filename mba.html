Eligibility

» Degree with minimum 50% marks( OBC 45% and SC/ST 40%) from a recognised university .Those who are studying in final year /semester of degree courses are also eligible to apply.
» Applicants need to have valid CMAT/KMAT/CAT Score.
» Group discussions and interview will be conducted for final selection of the candidates.
» Selection will be strictly on merit basis.
» Candidate must be citizen of India.

Documents to be produced at the time of admission:

1.SSLC/ Secondary Examination certificate in original for proof of age; with two self-attested copies.

2. Mark List and Pass Certificate of Plus II / Equivalent in original.
3. Two self-attested copies of the above Mark List and Pass Certificate.
4. Degree Certificate and Mark lists with two self-attested copies each.
5. Physical fitness certificate from a Government Medical Practitioner.
6. Transfer Certificate in original from the Institution last studied.
7. Course and conduct certificate from the Head of the Institution last studied.
8. Migration certificate in original, for students who have passed degree from Universities outside Kerala.
9. Eligibility certificate from KTU, for students who have passed degree from Universities outside Kerala.
10. Admit card of Entrance Examination in original.
11. Result sheet of the Entrance Examination in original.
12. Three passport size photographs.
13. Copy of Aadhaar card.
(In Case the candidate claims any sort of reservation or special consideration, documents to prove such claims.)
